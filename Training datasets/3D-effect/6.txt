3Deffect animates a raw brick facade with curved wooden shutters that project and undulate away from the window plane, introducing both sun control and texture. The double-layered masonry grid is perforated and articulated, lending a pixelated depth across the elevation. Located in a dry, dusty environment with minimal landscaping, the building embraces a grounded, earthy presence. Harsh sunlight emphasizes the geometry and shadows. The mood is expressive and bold, a tactile play of material performance.
